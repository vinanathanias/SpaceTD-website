OUR PROGRAMS

Zero Gravity Adventure

Experience the exhilaration of weightlessness aboard our zero gravity spacecraft. Float freely in a simulated microgravity environment and engage in thrilling astronaut training activities. Discover a new perspective on life as you defy gravity.

Flight Duration: 3 hours

International Space Station Grand Tour

Join an extended stay aboard the International Space Station (ISS). Experience life as an astronaut, conduct scientific experiments in microgravity, and gaze upon Earth from the orbital outpost. Immerse yourself in the extraordinary environment of space.

Flight Duration: 3 months
